CPP Art Department Logo

Objective: To create a new identity and brading for the Cal Poly Pomona Art Department using a new typeface.

Process: We started this project by creating a new typeface. First, we brainstormed creating many new typeface ideas, and narrowed down to one best typeface and then refined that one into our typeface to be used for the project. I used my typeface and filled in the counters and stretched certain letters to add more interest, then created a 3D aspect for clarity. Also created a clear space and style guide as part of the new branding.

Brainstorming Typeface Designs

Brainstorming lead to many different typeface ideas, and here are a few examples of this. The first is one of several different ideas I had for typefaces, and I decided to move forward with a simple box text with small square counters.

The next two are expansions of this idea of a simple rectangular shape, with rectangular counters to create the letters. The first example uses the same long rectangular shape to create the counters, in order to make a simple yet interesting rectangular letter form. The next was expanded further with even bigger rectangular counters.
